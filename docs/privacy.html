Privacy Policy

How Auto-Focus handles your data and protects your privacy

Last updated: January 31, 2025

The Simple Version

Auto-Focus is built privacy-first. The app runs completely offline on your Mac and doesn't collect or store your personal data. We only collect the minimum information needed to process purchases and manage licenses.

Data We Collect

Purchase Information

When you buy Auto-Focus+, we collect your email address and name through Stripe to send you your license key. This information is stored on our license server hosted by Hetzner in Germany.

License Verification

Auto-Focus+ periodically checks your license with our server. Only your license key is sent - no personal data, usage patterns, or app data is transmitted.

Website Analytics

We use self-hosted Plausible Analytics (hosted by Hetzner in Germany) to understand website usage. This data is not linked to purchases or personal information and respects your privacy.

Data We Don't Collect

  • Your focus sessions, apps, or productivity data
  • Websites you visit (Chrome extension data stays local)
  • Usage patterns or behavior tracking
  • Device information or system details
  • Any data from the Auto-Focus app itself

How Your Data is Protected

App Data

All your focus data, settings, and insights are stored locally on your Mac. You can export this data anytime in CSV or JSON format.

Chrome Extension

The extension monitors visited websites to detect focus apps but never stores or transmits this data. Website information is only used locally to match your configured focus URLs.

Server Security

Our license server uses industry-standard encryption and is hosted by Hetzner in Germany, subject to strict European data protection laws.

Third-Party Services

Stripe

Payment processing is handled by Stripe. See Stripe's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle payment data.

CDNs

Our website uses unpkg.com for Tailwind CSS and js.stripe.com for payment processing. These services may log IP addresses for security purposes.

Chrome Web Store

The Chrome extension is distributed through Google's Chrome Web Store, subject to Google's privacy policies.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your purchase information or license data by emailing help@auto-focus.app. Since the app stores data locally, you control all your focus and productivity data.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email us at help@auto-focus.app.
