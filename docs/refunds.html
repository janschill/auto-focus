Refund Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2026

14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a full refund within 14 days of purchase if Auto-Focus+ doesn't work as expected on your system or doesn't meet your needs.

How to Request a Refund

Email us at help@auto-focus.app within 14 days of purchase. Include:

  • • Your purchase email address
  • • Reason for the refund request
  • • Any issues you encountered (so we can improve)

Refund Process

Once approved, refunds are processed through Stripe and typically appear in your account within 5-10 business days, depending on your bank or card issuer.

Your Auto-Focus+ license will be deactivated when the refund is processed.

Try Before You Buy

Remember that Auto-Focus Free is available with core functionality, so you can test whether Auto-Focus works well on your system before purchasing Auto-Focus+.

Common Issues

Before requesting a refund, check our FAQ or email us for support. Many issues can be resolved quickly:

  • • macOS permissions not granted
  • • Do Not Disturb shortcut not installed
  • • License activation problems
  • • Chrome extension connectivity issues

Exceptions

Refunds may be declined for:

  • ✗ Requests after the 14-day period
  • ✗ Purchases made with invalid payment methods
  • ✗ Violations of our Terms of Service
