How It Works

1.

Background Monitoring: Auto-Focus continuously monitors which application is in the foreground, watching for your configured focus apps like VS Code, Xcode, or Figma.

2.

Timer Starts: When a focus app becomes active, a timer begins. Once you've been focused for your configured threshold (default: 12 minutes), Do Not Disturb automatically activates.

3.

Smart Buffer: Switch to Slack or Chrome briefly? No problem. The buffer time (default: 90 seconds) allows quick context switches without ending your focus session.

4.

Session Ends: When you leave focus apps for longer than the buffer time, the session ends and notifications are re-enabled automatically.
